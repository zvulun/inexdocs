Panel Wiring Diagram

Panel Wiring Legend

Item Description Ordering Information

A

Access Control Reader

Inex P/N: IZACR-QPR50

B

Reader-to-Panel Cable*: 4-conductor copper wires; use 22 AWG for up to 250 ft (76 m), or 18 AWG for 250-500 ft (152 m)

Supplied by integrator

C

Four-Door Access Control Panel

Inex P/N: IZACP-4D

D

Network Switch

Supplied by integrator

E

Cat6 LAN Cable**; Max. Length 330 ft (100 m)

Supplied by integrator

F

Power Supply for Access Panel: 12 VDC, 75W; DIN rail

Inex P/N: IZPWR75-12-MWL-DIN

G

Power Supply for Locks: 12/24 VDC (depends on lock type), 120W; DIN rail

  • Inex P/N: IZPWR120-12-MWL-DIN

  • Inex P/N: IZPWR120-24-MWL-DIN

H

Electric Lock

Supplied by integrator

I

Panel-to-Lock Cable (see AWG tables below)

Supplied by integrator

J

Sensor (to determine if door is opened or closed)

Supplied by integrator

K

Exit Button

Supplied by integrator

* Shielded cable of excellent quality in a noise free environment
** With Wi-Fi option, cable is not needed
