INEX Solution for Door Access Control

Comprehensive Door Access Control Solution (IZAC-QPR-1D)

INEX offers a comprehensive solution for Door Access Control consisting of several products.

The IZAC-QPR-1D Single Door Access Bundle from INEX offers a complete solution tailored for advanced single-door security, emphasizing QR code, PIN input, and RFID card access methods.

A typical system looks like this:


Reader (IZACR-QPR50)

A cutting-edge access control reader that recognizes QR codes, PIN inputs, and RFID cards.

  • Recognizes QR codes, PIN inputs and RFID cards

  • Integrated with the IZCloud Access Control system

  • Tempered glass for protection against vandalism

  • Wide temperature range - operating from -22°F to 158°F (-30°C to 70°C)

  • IP65 ingress protection against moisture and dust


Access Control Panel (IZACP-2D)

A specialized access control panel

  • Supports a wide variety of readers for QR codes, PIN inputs and RFID cards

  • Supports up to two doors

  • Integrated with IZCloud

  • Cloud connectivity RJ45 or WiFi


Metal Case (IZACA-MCPS)

Metal case accessory complete with a power supply unit. Designed to house and power INEX access control panels, this protective case ensures the hardware remains safe, secure, and consistently powered. Robust construction coupled with top-tier electrical components makes it the first choice for reliability and longevity in access control setups.
