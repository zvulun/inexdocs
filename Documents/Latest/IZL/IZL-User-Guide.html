3. Overview

3.1. Illuminator Models

The illuminator model codes are IZL[1/2]-(IR/DR/CL/WL)-10/20/30/H40/60, where:

  • After IZL:

  • 1/2 - number of LEDs: 1=24, 2=48

  • WVLN = Wavelength/type of LEDs:

    • IR - Infrared

    • DR - Deep Red

    • CL - Cool White (4000K)

    • WL - Warm White (3000K)

  • 10/20/30/H40/60 - LED beam angle, in degrees (Note: H40 = H40xV15)

5. Planning Your Installation

5.1. Matching an Illuminator to Your Camera System

The wavelength of an external illuminator must be compatible with the wavelength of the internal illuminators of the INEX camera. See the appropriate Camera Installation and Configuration Guide(s).

The number of illuminator LEDs and beam angle must match the distance rating of the camera being used, as follows:

  • Fewer LEDs and wider beam angles are used for short distances

  • More LEDs and narrower beam angles are used for longer distances

See the appropriate Camera Installation and Configuration Guide(s) for an explanation of the model number codes (effective distances, and wavelengths/number of LEDs).
