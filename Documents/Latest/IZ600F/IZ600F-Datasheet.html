IZ600F Datasheet

Bullet ALPR Camera

Rev. A

Key Features and Benefits

  • Designed for Parking, Access Control and Security

  • Compact design for flexible installation and ease of operation

  • Cost-effective solution with everything you need for ALPR

  • Motorized zoom and auto-focus – the key to optimized remote tuning

  • Wide temperature range – operating from -4 °F to 140 °F (-20°C to 60 °C)

  • IP67 ingress protection against extreme moisture

  • IK10 vandal-proof housing for efficient, worry-free operation

  • NDAA Section 889 compliant
