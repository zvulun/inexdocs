IZIDPUG Datasheet

Indoor AI Data Processing Unit

Rev. B

Key Features and Benefits

  • Designed for Parking, Car Wash, Access Control and Security

  • Supports up to 8 cameras - camera agnostic for maximum flexibility

  • NVIDIA® Artificial Intelligence (AI) GPU processor for lightning fast processing

  • RoadView video analytics for in-depth vehicle analysis

  • Integrated with IZCloud

  • Wide temperature range - operating from 32°F to 113°F (0°C to 45°C)

  • NDAA Section 889 compliant for our national security
